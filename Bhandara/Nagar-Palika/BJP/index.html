Biography

Early Life & Education

Shri Pankaj Rajesh Bhoyar is the Member of Legislative Assembly representing Bhandara District in Maharashtra. He has been actively working for the development of his constituency and the welfare of the people.

Shri Pankaj Rajesh Bhoyar was born and raised in Bhandara district, Maharashtra. From a young age, he showed keen interest in social service and community development. He completed his early education from local schools in Bhandara and pursued higher studies in political science and public administration.

Political Journey

Pankaj Bhoyar began his political career with the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and quickly rose through the ranks due to his dedication and connect with the people. He served in various organizational positions within the party before being elected as the Member of Legislative Assembly from Bhandara constituency.

His grassroots work and understanding of local issues made him a popular choice among voters. He has been instrumental in addressing the concerns of farmers, youth, and women in his constituency.

  • Active BJP member since early political career
  • Known for strong organizational skills
  • Excellent connect with youth and farmers
