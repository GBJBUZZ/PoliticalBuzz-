Biography

Shri Nitin Jairam Gadkari is the Union Minister for Road Transport & Highways, Government of India. He has been instrumental in transforming India's road infrastructure and promoting sustainable development.

*Source: Official Website of Shri Nitin Gadkari

Key Achievements

World-Class Highways

Successfully spearheaded the construction of record-breaking lengths of national highways and expressways.

Green Energy & Ethanol

Championing the use of alternative fuels like Ethanol and Hydrogen to reduce pollution and imports.

River Transport

Focused on developing inland waterways as an efficient and cost-effective mode of transport.

Smart City Projects

Integrated sustainable infrastructure and smart technology solutions into urban development.
